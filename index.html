The 5 Coolest Mechs in Animation and Their Pilots

The Mechs

  • Eva Unit 01
  • Omnimon
  • Gurren Lagann
  • Gundam Deathscythe
  • Iron Giant

Eva 01 from "Neon Genesis Evangelion"

Pilot(s):Shinji Ikari

Piloted by 14 yr old Shinji Ikari the EVA Unit 01 is a sleek and human like mech. When piloted correctly and with weapons like the "progressive knife" it's almost unstoppable. It also has cool powers like limb regeneration and a berserker rage.

Omnimon from "Digimon Adventure 2: Our War Game!"

Pilot(s):Tai Kamiya and Matt Ishida

Omnimon is a mech-like digimon that actually isn't piloted by anyone but is actually two digimon combined into one. They are powered by the power of friendship of humans Tai and Matt! Ominimon looks like an ethereal white knight with a giant sword and arm cannon to match.

Gurren Lagann from "Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann"

Pilot(s):Simon and Kamina

Piloted by Simon and Kamina Gurren Lagann is a giant combiner mech stolen from their enemies. This mech has a giant drill as his weapon and giant sunglasses on his torso. It can also transform and asorb other mechs to increase it's power!

Gundam Deathscythe from "Gundam Wing"

Pilot(s):Duo Maxwell

The Gundam Deathscythe looks like a Winged Grim Reaper. To be honest that's why it makes it on this list and will everytime. I mean just look at it! It also has a giant sycthe hence the name "Deathscythe" and is piloted by Duo Maxwell.

Iron Giant from "The Iron Giant"

Pilot(s):Hogarth Hughes

The Iron Giant is exactly his namesake, a giant robot made out of Iron. Found by the young boy Hogarth he becomes a friend and hero to all in Rockwell, Maine. He's also voiced by Vin Diesel which makes him super cool.
